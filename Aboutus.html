INTRODUCTION

Wilson High Secondary school is a private christian school mixed with both boys and girls in both ordinary and advanced levels of Education. We offer both Arts and Sciences including a number of co-curricular activities in our campus.

OUR MISSION

To develop academic and other talents in a student. Because Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it. We also see independent students who later create jobs instead of seek jobs.

Academic background
